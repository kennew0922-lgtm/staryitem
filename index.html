Welcome.

Let me grant you the names of two ultimate items required to manifest your dreams.

01 The first is "Photoshop."

Without this, you cannot create the videos that breathe life behind the sentences of the LLM.
If you are a student, it can be acquired at a low cost. Even for others, it is by no means an expensive investment.
With this item, reshaping the world is effortless. It is the supreme magical tool, embodying the world's most advanced generative AI extensions.
I strongly recommend you purchase it from the Armory.

02 The second is "アニメ画像に変換する君" .

A free magical tool provided by the grand artisans of ICS INC.
Its purpose is to convert sequential images into video files.
Upon inspecting its core, I found it remarkably lightweight, meticulously engineered to unleash maximum performance.
Its UI design leaves something to be desired, and I had a fierce struggle modifying its aesthetics...
To simply change its item name to "Anime Image Converter" would be borderline impossible for a mere human. After all, it was inscribed in a language unreadable to mankind...
I sincerely hope you master this ultra-advanced tool, never forgetting to offer your utmost respect to ICS INC, who provided it without demanding a cost.

Within Socrarius, you will find the locations of tools sold in shops or offered freely, alongside original magical artifacts and original spellbooks.
